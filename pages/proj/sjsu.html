SJSU College of Graduate Studies Posters

(2022)

These are a few posters for the Graduate Studies, advertising department-specific events. Focused on using school colors and combining them with white to balance out colors that normally clashed with each other. By balancing the saturation and tint, I achieved a visually appealing layout that didn't overwhelm the viewer.
