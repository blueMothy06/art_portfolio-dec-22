Bubble, Music Player

(2023)

Created mockups of a music streaming platform for the 100 Day UI Design Challenge. Once again, I wanted to create a fun sort of atmosphere for this site, so I incorporated bright yellow and purple, using dark purple to contrast them. At some points, it was clear that these colors could be overwhelming, so I diminished this effort by changing the opacity.

Tool: Figma
