Hey there!

Based in San Francisco, Catherine Luong is a digital artist whose work spans from game making, film, and illustration. As a lover of heavy stylization and all things atmospheric, she aims to create artwork that pulls viewers into a new world and makes them want to stay to the end. She has been a reader since she was a child, so she had always loved telling stories through her work. Catherine’s work is composed of stories, either exploring topics of loneliness and anxiety or fun, silly projects that aim to get a laugh out of someone. As a writer and illustrator, she enjoys turning her stories into games and plans to continue this into the future. Currently, she is a graduating senior at San Jose State University.
